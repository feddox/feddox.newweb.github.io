CHIKEN SOUVLAKI SALAD INGREDIENTS:

FOR THE GRILLED CHICKEN SOUVLAKI:

  • 1 pound boneless and skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into bite sized pieces
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 lemon, juice and zest
  • 1 tablespoon yogurt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

    • FOR THE SALAD:

  • 6 cups romaine lettuce, sliced
  • 1 cup tomato, diced
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup kalamata olives, pitted and coarsely chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup creamy feta and dill dressing or tzatziki sauce

    • DIRECTIONS

    FOR THE GRILLED CHIKEN SOUVLAKI

  • Marinate the chicken in the mixture of the remaining ingredients in the fridge for 30 minutes to overnight.
  • Grill the chicken over medium-high heat until cooked, about 2-4 minutes per side.

    • FOR THE SALAD

  • Assemble the salad and enjoy!
